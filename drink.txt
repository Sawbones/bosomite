Long Island Ice Tea	 Mix ingredients together over ice in a glass. Pour into a shaker and give one brisk shake. Pour back into the glass and make sure there is a touch of fizz at the top. Garnish with lemon.	 1 part Vodka 
1 part tequila 
1 part rum 
1 part gin 
part triple sec 
1 1/2 part sweet and sour mix 
splash of cola for color
